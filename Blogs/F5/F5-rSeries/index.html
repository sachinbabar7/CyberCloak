How rSeries Hardware Works

Next-generation hardware with modern FPGAs to enable SSL offloading and hardwar support for elliptical curve cryptography (ECC) ciphers in a 1 RU form factor.

  • rSeries hardwares:

    • Following are the available hardware models:

  • r10000 Series - Large Enterprise

    • Multi-Tenancy: 36 Tenants, Hardware Offload SSL/TLS: 200K TPS (RSA 2k keys), 140K TPS (ECDHE-ECDSA P-256), 110K TPS (ECDHE P-256-RSA 2k), 95 Gbps bulk encryption

  • r5000 Series - Mid-to-Large Enterprise

    • Multi-Tenancy: 18 Tenants, Hardware Offload SSL/TLS: 80K TPS (RSA 2k keys), 50K TPS (ECDHE-ECDSA P-256), 50K TPS (ECDHE P-256-RSA 2k), 45 Gbps bulk encryption

  • r4000 Series - Small-to-Medium Enterprise

    • Multi-Tenancy: 4 Tenants, Hardware Offload SSL/TLS: 45K TPS (RSA 2k keys), 20K TPS (ECDHE-ECDSA P-256), 20K TPS (ECDHE P-256-RSA 2k), 25 Gbps bulk encryption
